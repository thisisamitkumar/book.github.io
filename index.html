In The Search Of Heart..

Do love stories ever die?.... How would you react when a beautiful person comes into your life, and then goes away from you . . . forever ? Not all love stories are meant to have a perfect ending. Romantic, emotional and sincere, this This book, prancing around a race towards the seach of a perfect love story.
